How do You Know About This College







Subbalakshmi Lakshmipathy College of Science is an autonomous self-financing college affiliated to Madurai Kamaraj University and accredited with A+ Grade by NAAC. It is run by Subbalakshmi Lakshmipathy Foundation (SLF) a charitable trust initiated in 1989 to promote job-oriented education on a non-profit motive basis by Dr. R. Lakshmipathy, a doyen in the Newspaper industry. He is the publisher of the largest circulated Tamil Daily “Dinamalar” and also of the Medical Magazines, ‘The Antiseptic’ and the ‘Health’
